Piloting the survey has also allowed us to obtain information on likely hit rates when using the telephone survey methodology, which have informed plans to conduct the survey on a national level.

The importance of formal communication is confirmed since persons without formal communication were unable to provide information about immediate and external environments or request clarification. Training in formal communication may be of benefit in allowing clients to perform these skills.
